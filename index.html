Research

  • Utilizing Task-Generic Motion Prior to Recover Full-Body Motion from Very Sparse Signals (Myungjin Shin, Dohae Lee, In-Kwon Lee): Reconstruction of full body motion of a human given only signals from VR controllers and headset. I contributed to development of the algorithm, hyperparameter optimization and development of the ComfyUI model.
